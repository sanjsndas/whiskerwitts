Cookies Policy

Whisker Wits uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, improve user experience, and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them, while session cookies are temporary and are erased when you close your browser.

1.1 Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies on our website:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core features like page navigation, secure areas, and booking functionality. You cannot opt out of these cookies.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use tools like Google Analytics to improve our site.
  • Functional Cookies: These allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features.
  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies: These are used to deliver ads that are relevant to you and your interests. They also help us measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enable the core functionality of our website, including booking appointments and processing payments.
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve our services and content.
  • To personalize your experience by remembering your preferences and settings.
  • To deliver relevant advertisements and promotions through third-party platforms like Google and Facebook.
  • To enhance security and detect fraudulent activity.

3.1 Third-Party Cookies

We also allow third-party service providers to place cookies on your device to support our analytics, advertising, and social media integration. These include:

  • Google Analytics: Provides anonymized usage data to help us improve our website.

These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We do not control the cookies placed by these third parties.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block all cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

5. Cookie Retention Periods

The retention period for cookies varies depending on their purpose. Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for the duration specified in the cookie table above, or until you manually delete them. We regularly review our cookie usage and ensure that retention periods are appropriate.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't accept cookies?

You can decline non-essential cookies through our consent banner or your browser settings. However, some features of our website, such as booking appointments or remembering your preferences, may not work optimally.

Do you use cookies for marketing?

Yes, we use targeting cookies to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign performance. You can opt out of these cookies via the consent banner or your browser settings.

How can I delete cookies already stored on my device?

You can delete cookies through your browser settings. Please refer to the browser-specific links provided above for step-by-step instructions.

Do we use cookies to track you across other websites?

We use third-party analytics and advertising partners that may track your browsing across multiple sites to build a profile of your interests. This helps us show you more relevant content and ads.
