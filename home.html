Grooming Process – Step by Step

1. Consultation – We discuss your cat’s coat, health, and temperament.

2. Brushing & Detangling – Gentle removal of knots before the bath.

3. Warm Bath – Using soothing, fragrance‑free shampoos.

4. Condition & Rinse – Deep conditioning for extra shine.

5. Drying – Low‑noise dryers and plenty of treats.

6. Scissor Trim / Style – Precision cutting for your cat’s silhouette.

7. Finishing – Nail trim, ear clean, and a spritz of cat‑safe cologne.

8. Purr‑check – We ensure your cat is relaxed before you pick them up.

Safety & Hygiene

All equipment is sanitized between cats. We use hospital‑grade disinfectants and disposable liners. Our grooming tables are anti‑slip and padded. We are fully insured and certified in pet first aid. Your cat’s well‑being is our highest priority.

Whisker Wits was founded in 2016 with a simple mission: to make grooming a positive experience for every cat. Over the years, we have groomed over 5,000 cats, from playful kittens to wise seniors. Our methods are low‑stress, high‑reward – we use calming pheromones, gentle handling, and plenty of praise. We are proud members of the National Cat Groomers Association.

We understand that every cat is unique. That’s why we customize each session based on coat type, age, and personality. Whether your cat needs a simple bath or a full‑on makeover, we approach each groom with patience and artistry. Our clients often tell us their cats actually look forward to their appointments – that’s the Whisker Wits difference.

We also offer educational resources: free guides on home brushing, nail trimming tips, and ear care. Ask us about our “Groom & Learn” sessions where you can observe and ask questions. We believe an informed owner is the best partner in your cat’s grooming journey.

Our salon features a separate “quiet room” for cats who need extra calm. We use soft lighting, classical music, and Feliway diffusers. Every cat receives a personalized “paw‑print” certificate after their session.

We also host seasonal events: “Halloween Howl” costume grooming, “Winter Whisker” spa days, and “Spring Shed” specials. Follow us on social media for updates and adorable before‑after photos.
