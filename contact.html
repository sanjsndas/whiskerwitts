Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at +1 (212) 555‑9474, sending an email to hello@whiskerwits.com, or using the contact form above. We recommend booking at least 3–5 days in advance, especially during peak seasons.

Do you offer emergency grooming services?

Yes, we understand that emergencies happen. If your cat has a severe matting issue, a skin condition, or an urgent grooming need, please call us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate you as soon as possible.

Can I bring my cat for a free consultation?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary 15‑minute consultations to assess your cat's coat condition, discuss your grooming goals, and answer any questions. Just call or email us to schedule a consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and cash. We also offer payment plans for our membership packages.

Do you offer discounts for multiple cats?

Yes! We offer a 10% discount on grooming services for households with two or more cats. Ask about our multi‑cat packages when you book.

Is there parking available near the salon?

Yes, there is metered street parking available directly outside our location. We're also conveniently located near the F and G subway lines, just a 5‑minute walk from the Bergen Street station.

At Whisker Wits, we believe that every cat deserves a spa experience that's both relaxing and rejuvenating. Our team of certified feline groomers combines expertise with genuine love for cats. We pride ourselves on our transparent communication, fair pricing, and commitment to excellence. Our salon is clean, quiet, and designed specifically for feline comfort – no barking dogs, just peaceful purrs.
